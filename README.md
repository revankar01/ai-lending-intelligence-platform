# AI Lending Intelligence Platform

A decision intelligence tool for NBFC–Insurance partnerships.

This platform simulates how lenders can embed insurance products during loan origination using borrower attributes and risk signals.

## Features

• Borrower risk scoring engine  
• Insurance attach probability modelling  
• NBFC partner revenue simulation  
• Portfolio revenue simulation  
• AI-generated distribution strategy insights  
• Interactive analytics dashboard

## Example Use Case

An NBFC can evaluate:

- probability of insurance attach
- recommended protection products
- revenue share for lender vs insurer
- portfolio level premium opportunity

## Why I Built This

While managing ₹100Cr+ NBFC insurance partnerships, I often noticed that insurance cross-sell was driven more by distribution pressure than intelligence.

This tool demonstrates how embedded analytics can improve attach rates, product fit and partner revenue outcomes.
